About The Art of Idea

Coming up with ideas is easy — even good ideas are a dime a dozen.

Materialized ideas, a.k.a. those that are acted upon, are much harder to arrive at. There is an art to materializing ideas.


art

craft   skill   artifice   science   workmanship  
noun

The expression or application of human creative skill and imagination, typically in a visual form such as painting or sculpture,...: "the art of the Renaissance"

noun

Works produced by such skill and imagination.


idea

notion thought concept opinion view
noun

A thought or suggestion as to a possible course of action: "the idea of linking pay to performance".

noun

A concept or mental impression.
